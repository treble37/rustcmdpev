# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and professional collaboration. Contributors should:

- Assume good intent while reviewing technical work.
- Give concrete feedback tied to code, behavior, or requirements.
- Avoid harassment, insults, intimidation, and discriminatory language.
- Respect differing experience levels and communication styles.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or sustained hostility.
- Harassment, stalking, or unwanted sexual attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately disruptive behavior in issues, pull requests, or community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content that violates this policy and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

Open a private maintainer contact channel before filing a public report when the report itself contains sensitive personal details.
